## App Name
Space Farm: BioGel Empire

## Short Description
Build a space farm, deploy drones, and terraform a new world.

## Full Description
Start from a tiny biodome and grow it into a full-scale terraforming empire.

Your core loop is simple:
- collect BioGel by tapping,
- invest in upgrades,
- automate production with drones,
- scale your economy and unlock advanced tech.

### Features
- Idle clicker progression with steady growth.
- Upgrade paths for tap power, passive income, and global multiplier.
- Offline progress: your farm keeps producing while you are away.
- Clean economy design focused on progression and readability.
- Space-farming theme with domes, orbital modules, and terraforming systems.

### Rewarded-only monetization
- No auto-play ads.
- No forced interstitials or app-open ads.
- Rewards are granted only when the player taps the ad button.

Optimize your production chain, power up your drone fleet, and turn a barren world into a thriving biosphere.
